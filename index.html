Statement of Purpose

As a student majoring in Multimedia Communication at VinUniversity and a passionate content creator on TikTok, I have always loved telling stories through visuals. My long-term dream is to work professionally in the media industry, creating impactful campaigns that resonate with audiences. Currently, while I am still studying, I want to step into the real world and gain practical experience through internships and part-time jobs. This portfolio is my way of preparing for that journey, serving as a bridge between my life as a student creator and my future as a professional media practitioner. My goal is to use these documents to show potential employers that I am ready to work.

I have chosen the Job Seeking Package because my immediate priority is to apply for jobs that fit my schedule and skills. To achieve this, I have included five specific artifacts: a creative yet professional CV that highlights my multimedia experience, a tailored Cover Letter that explains my passion, Email Templates for networking, and Thank-You Notes for post-interview follow-ups. Each document is designed with a specific audience in mind—recruiters and hiring managers. I want to prove to them that even though I am a student, I possess the discipline and professionalism required for the job. These documents are not just assignments; they are the actual tools I will use to knock on career doors.

Looking back at my time in this course, I feel a deep sense of accomplishment that I did not expect. To be honest, I used to be very unconfident about my English writing skills. When I first saw that we had to write a Literature Review, I was terrified; I genuinely did not believe I could complete such a difficult academic task because I did not think my English was good enough. However, Professor Jon’s interesting and detailed lectures changed everything for me. He taught us with such dedication, focusing on even the smallest details—from capitalization rules to exactly where to put commas in the reference list. These specific lessons helped me realize that professional writing is about care, structure, and attention to detail, not just about knowing big words.

Thanks to this course, I have overcome my fear of writing and moved from self-doubt to feeling capable. I am truly grateful to Professor Jon for his enthusiastic guidance, and I also want to send a special thank you to Ms. Lan, our Teaching Assistant. Her support and feedback helped me feel more confident in my work. Completing the Literature Review gave me the belief that I can handle difficult tasks if I put my heart into them. This portfolio is proof of my growth, and I am now ready to send these documents to future employers with pride and confidence.
